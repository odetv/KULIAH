Latihan
Tipe data 1 byte
Dapatkan bentuk binernya

Soal 1
-9 = ~9 + 1
-9 = ~(0000 1001) + 1
-9 = 0000 1001
     --------- NOT
     1111 0110
             1
     --------- +
     1111 0111 = -9

Soal 2
-20 = ~20 + 1
-20 = ~(0001 0100) + 1
-20 = 0001 0100
      --------- NOT
      1110 1011
              1
      --------- +
      1110 1100 = -20

Soal 3
-30 = ~30 + 1
-30 = ~(0001 1110) + 1
-30 = 0001 1110
      --------- NOT
      1110 0001
              1
      --------- +
      1110 0010 = -30
